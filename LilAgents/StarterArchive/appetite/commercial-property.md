## Coverage Forms & Features

- **Special form and replacement cost** available for eligible buildings
- **Wind deductibles** as low as 1%
- **Coastal properties** accepted
- Buildings dating to **1850** considered with documented system updates
- Earthquake coverage available (excludes Oklahoma and Kansas)
- Business Personal Property up to $250K per building (retail)

## Eligible Verticals

### Monoline Property
Top quotable occupancies: 1–4 family dwellings, apartments, hotels/motels, restaurants, offices, retail/mercantile, warehouses, healthcare facilities, schools, daycares, jewelry stores, electronics stores, lessor's risk.

### Lessor's Risk / LRO
- Up to 1,000 residential units including apartments, student housing, tract housing, HUD, and assisted living
- Wind-exposed coastal property accepted
- Buildings from 1850+ with complete system upgrades within 30 years

### Vacant Buildings
- Properties less than 30% occupied
- No square footage limitations
- No strict story limit for fully sprinklered buildings
- Terms: 3, 6, 9, or 12 months
- Theft and vandalism coverage available

### Restaurants, Bars & Taverns
- Must have commercial cooking equipment with semi-annual cleaning contracts, or UL300-compliant ANSUL fire suppression system
- Enhanced limits available in southern states

### Retail & Services
- Business Personal Property up to $250K per building
- Barber shops, beauty parlors, nail salons, grocery stores, liquor stores
