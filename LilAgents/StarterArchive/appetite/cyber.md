## Who Should Have Cyber Coverage?

Any business that handles any of the following is a strong candidate:
- Customer personal information (names, addresses, dates of birth)
- Credit card or payment data
- Digital assets and intellectual property
- Cloud storage or SaaS systems
- Email communications
- Healthcare or financial data

## Ineligible Classes

- Cryptocurrency businesses
- Adult entertainment
- Gambling businesses
