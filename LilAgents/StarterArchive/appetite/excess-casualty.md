## How Excess Casualty Works

Excess casualty (also called umbrella or excess liability) sits above your client's primary general liability or LRO policy and responds once primary limits are exhausted. Pathpoint writes excess over most A-rated primary carriers and Lloyd's syndicates.

**Example:** A contractor with a $1M/$2M primary GL policy can add a $4M excess policy through Pathpoint, giving them $5M total limits.

## Combined Coverage Options

Excess casualty is often quoted alongside:
- **Contractors GL** — primary GL + excess for contractors needing higher limits
- **Lessor's Risk** — primary LRO package + excess for commercial property owners

The platform supports quoting primary and excess together in one submission.
