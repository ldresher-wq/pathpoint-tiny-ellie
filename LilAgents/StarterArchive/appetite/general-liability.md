## Eligible Verticals

### Contractors
- Annual revenues up to $5M
- Per-project aggregate up to $5M
- Hired & non-owned auto available
- Tools/equipment: $5K–$10K

### Lessor's Risk (LRO)
- GL, property, or commercial package options
- Excess liability up to $5M
- Apartments, commercial buildings, retail, mixed-use

### Vacant Buildings
- Terms of 3, 6, 9, or 12 months available
- Buildings constructed after 1850 with documented updates

### Restaurants, Bars & Taverns
- Up to $2M/$2M general liability limit
- Requires UL300-compliant wet ANSUL system over cooking equipment, OR semi-annual cleaning contract

### Manufacturing
- Annual revenues up to $5M
- Products liability included up to $2M
- Classes: cosmetics, candles, food and beverage, clothing, shoes, leather goods, and 500+ more

### Retail & Services
- Products-completed operations liability included
- Inland marine for food trucks: $50K any one item/$100K per schedule
- Classes: food trucks, salons, grocery, liquor stores, retail shops
