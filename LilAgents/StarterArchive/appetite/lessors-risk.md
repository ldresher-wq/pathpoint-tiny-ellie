## Eligible Property Classes (Top Quotable)

Apartments, banks, bars and taverns, commercial buildings, condominiums, daycares, dwellings, mixed-use buildings, offices, parking facilities and garages, restaurants, retail and storefronts, shopping centers, vacation rentals, warehouses.

## Property Features

- **Coastal and wind-exposed properties** accepted
- Buildings dating to **1850** with complete system upgrades within 30 years
- Wind deductibles as low as 1%
- Up to **1,000 residential units** — including apartments, student housing, tract housing, HUD, and assisted living facilities
- Special form and replacement cost available for eligible buildings

## Considered (Higher-Risk) Classes

The following are considered on a case-by-case basis and may be referred to underwriting:
- New ventures without prior coverage history
- Retail and habitational tenancies
- Properties with prior loss history
- Restaurant tenants with commercial cooking equipment
- Legal marijuana distribution or retail tenants
- Homeowner or condo associations with amenities (playgrounds, hot tubs, sports courts)
