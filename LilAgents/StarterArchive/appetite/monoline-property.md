Pathpoint's monoline property program provides standalone commercial property coverage for a wide range of occupancy types, including coastal properties and older buildings, with instant-quotable options for most standard submissions.

## Coverage Highlights

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| TIV (standard) | Up to $3.8M per location / $5.8M per policy (Instant Quotable) |
| TIV (AL, FL, GA, LA, MS, SC, TX) | Up to $10M (Instant Quotable) |
| TIV (higher limits) | Up to $30M |
| Building Age | Dating to 1850 with documented system updates |
| Coastal Properties | Accepted |
| Wind Deductible | As low as 1% |
| Coverage Form | Special form and replacement cost available for eligible buildings |
| Earthquake | Available (excludes Oklahoma and Kansas) |
| Monoline Wind | Available with 1%–5% deductible options |

## Eligible Occupancy Classes

1–4 family dwellings, apartments, hotels and motels, restaurants, offices, retail and mercantile, warehousing and storage, healthcare facilities, schools and daycares, jewelry stores, electronics stores, lessor's risk occupancies.

## Key Features

- **Special form** (open perils) and **replacement cost** valuation available for eligible buildings
- **Coastal and wind-exposed** properties accepted — wind deductibles as low as 1%
- **Older buildings** from 1850+ accepted with documentation of system updates within the past 30 years
- **Earthquake coverage** available in most states (excludes OK, KS)
- **Monoline wind** option with deductible choices of 1%–5%
- Non-admitted insurance provided through a licensed surplus lines broker

## How to Quote

Submit at app.pathpoint.com. Select the **Monoline Property** vertical. Standard submissions receive an instant quote within seconds.
