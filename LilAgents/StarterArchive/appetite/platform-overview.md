Pathpoint is a digital wholesale insurance platform that enables independent retail agents to quote and bind Excess & Surplus (E&S) small commercial insurance through a fully automated portal — without needing a direct relationship with surplus lines carriers.

## What Is E&S Insurance?

Excess & Surplus (E&S) lines insurance covers risks that standard (admitted) carriers won't write — hard-to-place businesses, higher-hazard operations, unusual property types, or accounts with loss history. Pathpoint gives retail agents direct access to E&S markets that were previously only accessible through traditional wholesalers.

## Key Platform Features

- **Instant quotes** via automated underwriting — 80% of submissions receive an instant quote
- **Average submission time:** ~7 minutes
- **Quote turnaround:** Seconds for instant quotes; within 4 hours for referred accounts
- **24/7 access** for quoting, document management, and account tracking
- **Single submission to multiple markets** — one submission reaches 27+ AM Best A-rated carriers
- **E-signature** capability for subjectivities
- **AI-assisted pre-fill** — upload an ACORD or describe operations and the form fills automatically
- **Integrated payment processing** via PathPay (100% digital, first in the E&S industry)
- **Unified supplemental documentation** requirements
