## Fire Suppression Requirement

**All restaurants with commercial cooking equipment must have one of the following:**
- A UL300-compliant wet ANSUL fire suppression system over all cooking equipment, **OR**
- A semi-annual commercial kitchen cleaning contract

This is a hard underwriting requirement — risks without one of these cannot be quoted.

## Eligible Classes (Top Quotable)

- Restaurants without alcohol sales
- Restaurants with alcohol sales (up to a specified percentage)
- BYOB establishments
- Fast food and quick service operations
- Restaurants by concessionaires
- Establishments with commercial cooking equipment meeting ANSUL/cleaning requirements

## Considered Risks (May Require Referral)

- Fine dining through quick service formats
- BBQ pits and smokers located 100+ feet from the premises
- Table-side cooking (where patrons are not cooking)
- Hired non-owned auto (no delivery, valet, livery, or food truck operations)

## Ineligible States

Coverage is **not available** in Alaska, Hawaii, or Washington D.C.
