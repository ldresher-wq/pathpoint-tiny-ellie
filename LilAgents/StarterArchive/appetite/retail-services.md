## Food Truck Specific Coverage

Food trucks have specialized coverage options:
- **Inland Marine:** $50K any one item / $100K per schedule
- **Equipment & supplies:** $1,500 per item
- **Business income and spoilage** coverage available
- **Liquor liability required** when serving alcohol
- **Auto liability required** at equal or greater limits
- Must comply with all applicable permits and licenses
- Up to **30% alcohol sales** accepted (with liquor liability coverage)

## Considered Risks

- New ventures accepted (no minimum time in business)
- No maximum sales for salons and food trucks
- Convenience/grocery stores up to $3M gross sales
- General retail stores up to $5M gross sales
