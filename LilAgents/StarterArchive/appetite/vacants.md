Pathpoint writes general liability and property coverage for vacant buildings and vacant land, including properties under renovation and coastal locations.

## Coverage Highlights

| Coverage | Limit |
|---|---|
| General Liability | $500K–$2M (Instant Quotable) |
| Property TIV (standard) | Up to $2.5M (Instant Quotable) |
| Property TIV (higher limits) | Up to $50M |

## Key Appetite Features

- **Properties less than 30% occupied** accepted
- **No square footage limitations**
- **No strict story limit** for fully sprinklered buildings
- **Buildings dating to 1850** with documented updates considered
- **Coastal properties** accepted
- **Properties under renovation** eligible (cosmetic renovations not exceeding half of TIV)
- **Theft and vandalism coverage** available
- **Additional Insured coverage** available

## Available Terms

Vacant policies are available in 3, 6, 9, or 12-month terms — ideal for properties between tenants, under renovation, or awaiting sale.

## Eligible Ownership Types

Individuals, LLCs, trusts, estates, and other ownership structures are all considered.

## Eligible Property Classes

Vacant apartments, coastal properties, commercial buildings, factories, habitational properties, dwellings and homes, land (for-profit and not-for-profit), office buildings, restaurants, residential buildings, retail buildings, warehouses.

## Coverage Scope

- **General Liability:** Available for both vacant buildings and vacant land
- **Property:** Available for vacant buildings only (not land)
