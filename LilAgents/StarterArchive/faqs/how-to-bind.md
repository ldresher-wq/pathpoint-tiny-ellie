Pathpoint streamlines the binding process through five key steps. Here is a complete walkthrough.

## Step 1: Appointment & PathPay Setup (One-Time)
Before binding your first account, complete the following one-time setup:
- Navigate to **Settings** via your profile name
- Click **Start Appointment** (takes approximately two minutes)
- Scroll to the **Billing** section and create your PathPay account
- Verify your account if prompted

## Step 2: Select a Quote
- Log in and go to the Overview page
- Select the account under the Submissions tab
- Review and select preferred quote(s)
- Adjust pricing if needed using the adjustment icon
- Review cover letters and finance estimates in the side panel
- Click **Continue to Subjectivities**

## Step 3: Complete Subjectivities
Agents choose between two signing methods:

### E-Sign Method (Fastest)
1. Enter insured contact information
2. Select inspection and payment contacts
3. Complete all subjectivities
4. Click **Request to Bind**
5. The insured receives an email to review and sign
6. Receive underwriter confirmation within four business hours

### Manual Sign Method
1. Download the subjectivities packet
2. Complete and manually sign all documents
3. Verify signatures and payment information accuracy
4. Indicate which party is responsible for payment
5. Upload completed documents
6. Click **Request to Bind**

## Step 4: Confirm Payment
Payment must be confirmed before the policy activates:
- The billing party receives a payment link within 24 hours of underwriter approval
- Payment options: credit card, wire transfer, or ACH
- Payment must be received within 30 business days to honor the effective date
- Choose between **PathPay Agency** or **PathPay Insured** billing
- Coverage cannot bind without payment confirmation

## Step 5: Download Policy Documents
Once the account displays **Issued**:
- Access policy documents on the main Account Page
- Find documents in the right-side panel
- Download as needed

## Correcting Subjectivities
If underwriters request corrections:
- You will receive an email notification with required changes
- Underwriter comments appear highlighted in red in the portal
- Make the necessary corrections
- Click **Resubmit the bind request**
- Underwriters respond within four business hours

## Key FAQs

**Can I reverse a bind request once submitted?**
No. Once initiated, agents cannot independently reverse a binding request — underwriter approval is required.

**Why was my subjectivities packet declined?**
Common reasons include unsigned forms or missing state-required digital verification stamps.

**What if I am quoting Mix & Match (multiple carriers)?**
Two supplemental packets are required when selecting coverage from multiple carriers separately.

**Can I bind without payment confirmation?**
No. Coverage cannot bind until payment is confirmed for either billing method.
