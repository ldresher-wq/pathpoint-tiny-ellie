Pathpoint's quoting process is fully digital and typically takes about 7 minutes to complete a submission.

## Step-by-Step Quoting Process

### 1. Create an Account
New users should visit Pathpoint.com and click **Create an account**. Follow the setup prompts to get started.

### 2. Start Your Submission
Select the vertical that best matches your client's business, then choose a coverage type:
- General Liability
- Commercial Property
- Commercial Package
- Excess Casualty
- Cyber Liability

The platform offers a **Coverage Finder** tool and AI-assisted pre-fill — upload an ACORD or SOV, or describe the client's operations in a text field for automatic form completion.

### 3. Upload Documents
Submit supporting materials such as ACORDs and Schedules of Values (SOVs), or describe the client's business in a text field for automatic form completion.

### 4. Complete the Application
Review the pre-filled fields and answer any remaining questions. A progress dial displays your submission completion status.

### 5. Review & Submit
Verify accuracy and submit to available markets. Results appear immediately showing whether the risk qualifies for:
- **Instant quoting** — quote appears on-screen within seconds
- **Referred to underwriter** — underwriting team contacts you within 4 business hours
- **Declined** — submission does not meet appetite

### 6. Check Quote Status
Instant quotes appear on-screen. Referred cases receive underwriter contact within 4 hours of submission.

### 7. Request to Bind
Once approved, request binding directly within Pathpoint. For assistance, contact ops@pathpoint.com or your Account Manager.

## Quick Start for Returning Users
The platform remembers previous submissions, enabling faster processing for returning users through the Quick Start functionality.

## Tips for Faster Processing
- Complete all fields accurately before submitting
- Upload supporting documents (ACORDs, loss runs, SOVs) upfront
- Use the Coverage Finder to select the right vertical
- Monitor your inbox for emails marked **ACTION NEEDED**
