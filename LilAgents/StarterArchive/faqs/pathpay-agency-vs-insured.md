---
title: "What is the difference between PathPay Agency and PathPay Insured?"
date: "2026-04-28"
type: "faq"
category: "Billing & Payments"
---

Pathpoint offers two billing options through PathPay. The core difference is **who is responsible for paying Pathpoint** — the agency or the insured directly.

## PathPay Insured

The insured pays Pathpoint directly. The agency is removed from the payment collection process entirely.

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Who pays Pathpoint | The insured |
| Payment methods | Credit card, wire transfer, ACH, installment plans |
| Payment deadline | 3 business days after subjectivities are approved |
| Coverage binding | Does NOT bind until payment is received |
| Commission payment | Automatic — weekly or monthly |
| Agency financial risk | Low — agency is never liable for the premium |

**Best for:** Agencies that want to reduce accounts-receivable risk and simplify their billing process.

## PathPay Agency

The agency collects the premium from the insured and remits payment to Pathpoint. This mirrors the traditional wholesale billing model.

| Feature | Detail |
|---|---|
| Who pays Pathpoint | The agency |
| Settlement deadline | 20 calendar days to settle the net balance |
| Payment methods | Credit card, ACH, check (for new users) |
| Billing tracking | Real-time dashboard |
| Agency financial risk | Higher — agency is responsible for the full net premium |

**Best for:** Agencies that prefer to maintain their traditional billing relationship with the insured and have strong accounts-receivable management.

## Key Consideration: Timing

With **PathPay Agency**, clicking **Request to Bind** initiates automatic binding. If you are waiting for the insured to send funds before remitting, avoid clicking this button prematurely — once activated, binding cannot be reversed without underwriter approval.

With **PathPay Insured**, the account does not bind until the insured completes their payment, so there is no risk of binding before funds are received.

## Additional Fees

There may be additional fees associated with credit card payment processing. Contact your Account Manager or email ops@pathpoint.com for current fee schedules.

## Commission Visibility

To see total commissions received from Pathpoint, navigate to **Billing & Commissions** in the platform dashboard.
