## Quote Speed at Pathpoint

Pathpoint is designed for speed. Here are the typical timelines:

| Stage | Timeframe |
|---|---|
| Submission completion | ~7 minutes average |
| Instant quote (eligible risks) | Seconds — appears on-screen immediately |
| Referred quote (underwriter review) | Within 4 business hours of submission |
| Bind request review | Within 4 business hours |
| Policy documents after bind | Same day in most cases |

**80% of submissions are instantly quoted**, typically within 60 seconds via automated underwriting.

## What Determines Whether a Quote Is Instant?

Instant quoting is available for risks that fall within Pathpoint's standard appetite parameters. Risks that fall outside standard parameters — or involve higher complexity — are referred to an underwriter for manual review.

The platform shows immediately upon submission whether your risk will be:
- **Instantly quoted** — quote appears on screen
- **Referred** — underwriting team contacts you within 4 hours
- **Declined** — does not meet current appetite

## Tips to Speed Up Your Submission

1. **Upload supporting documents upfront** — ACORDs, loss runs, and SOVs reduce back-and-forth
2. **Select the correct vertical** — starting in the right product category avoids delays
3. **Answer all questions accurately** — incomplete fields may trigger referrals
4. **Monitor your inbox** — watch for ACTION NEEDED emails and respond promptly
5. **Provide recent updates** — current financial records, website links, and relevant claim history

## Quote Expiration

Most carrier quotes remain valid for **30–45 days**. If a quote expires, you will need to refresh the submission to obtain a current, bindable quote.

## Resubmitting After a Declination

If a submission is declined, you can edit and resubmit. Contact your Account Manager or email ops@pathpoint.com for guidance on how to strengthen the submission.
