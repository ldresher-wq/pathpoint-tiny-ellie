## Accessing Renewals in Pathpoint

Renewals are managed directly within the Pathpoint platform. Here is how to find and work with renewing accounts:

## Finding Your Renewals

1. Log in to Pathpoint at app.pathpoint.com
2. Navigate to the **Submissions** tab on your Overview page
3. Filter or sort accounts by expiration date to identify upcoming renewals
4. Renewing accounts will display their status and expiration date

## The Renewal Process

Pathpoint Underwriting handles renewal processing on your behalf. For most accounts:
- You will receive renewal notification ahead of expiration
- Review the renewal terms and updated pricing
- Complete any updated subjectivities if required
- Confirm payment to bind the renewal

## Renewal Timelines

- Begin reviewing renewals **45–60 days** before expiration
- Most carrier quotes are valid for 30–45 days
- Do not let a renewal quote expire — you will need to resubmit if it does

## If a Renewal Is Not Automatically Offered

Some accounts may not receive an automatic renewal offer if:
- The risk has had significant losses
- The account has fallen outside the carrier's current appetite
- Required documentation was not updated

In these cases, contact your Account Manager or submit the account fresh through the platform to find alternative markets.

## Cancellations vs. Non-Renewals

- **Cancellation:** Policy is terminated mid-term. Contact ops@pathpoint.com for assistance.
- **Non-renewal:** Carrier declines to renew at expiration. Pathpoint can help find alternative markets.
