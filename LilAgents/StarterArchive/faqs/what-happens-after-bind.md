After you click **Request to Bind** in Pathpoint, here is exactly what happens next.

## Immediate Confirmation
You receive a **Bind Request Received** confirmation on-screen when submitting the request.

## Underwriter Review
Underwriters review the submission within **four business hours**, after which:
- Agents receive confirmation of approval
- The billing contact receives a payment link

## E-Sign Path
For electronic signatures, you will see an in-app alert indicating the insured's signature is pending. Once the insured completes the electronic signing, the request officially activates and the on-screen confirmation message appears.

## Manual Sign Path
When using manual signatures, agents must collect the insured's signatures before submitting through the dashboard. Upon submission, the request immediately registers with the confirmation message displayed.

## Important: Binding Cannot Be Reversed
Once you click **Request to Bind**, any subsequent changes or cancellations **must be approved by an underwriter**. Agents cannot independently reverse a binding request.

## If the Bind Request Is Declined
If underwriting declines the request, agents receive an **ACTION NEEDED** email with required corrections. Follow the instructions to resubmit the subjectivities.

## After Binding: Policy Servicing
Pathpoint Underwriting handles account servicing post-bind, including:
- Calculating surplus lines taxes
- Filing required forms and affidavits
- Maintaining account compliance

Pathpoint files all surplus lines taxes on your behalf — you do not need to handle this separately.

## Accessing Policy Documents
Once the account status shows **Issued**, policy documents are available in the right-side panel of the main Account page.
