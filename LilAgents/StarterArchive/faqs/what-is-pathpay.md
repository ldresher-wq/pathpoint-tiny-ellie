## What Is PathPay?

PathPay is Pathpoint's 100% digital payment solution for E&S insurance — the first of its kind in the E&S industry. It eliminates paper invoices and manual check processing, giving agencies a modern, streamlined way to handle premium payments.

## Two PathPay Options

### PathPay Insured
The insured pays directly to Pathpoint, removing the agency from the payment collection process.

**Key features:**
- Agents share a payment link with the insured
- Insured can pay via credit card, wire transfer, ACH, or installment plan
- Coverage **does not bind** until payment is received
- Insureds have **three business days** to complete payment once subjectivities are approved
- Commissions are paid automatically — weekly or monthly
- Reduces agency financial risk since the agency is never on the hook for the premium

### PathPay Agency
The agency collects the premium and remits to Pathpoint, modernizing the traditional agency billing model while keeping the agency responsible for settlement.

**Key features:**
- Agency has **20 calendar days** to settle the net balance
- Payment tracking dashboard for real-time visibility
- Supports credit card, ACH, and check payments
- Agency maintains the traditional billing relationship with the insured

## Key Benefits of PathPay

- **First 100% digital payment method in the E&S industry**
- Streamlined processing from bind to policy issuance
- Payers can make a payment **without logging in** by entering the Policy Number and ZIP Code
- Transparent reporting through dashboards for real-time payment tracking
- Automatic commission payments
- Reduced administrative burden vs. traditional paper-based billing
- Integrated with Ascend for billing management

## Setting Up PathPay

To set up your PathPay account:
1. Navigate to **Settings** via your profile name in Pathpoint
2. Scroll to the **Billing** section
3. Click to create your PathPay account
4. Verify your account if prompted

This is a one-time setup required before you can bind your first account.

## Accessing Billing
To manage billing and payments, navigate to the **Billing & Commissions** tab within Pathpoint. This connects to your agency's Ascend account. New users need their agency administrator to grant them access.
