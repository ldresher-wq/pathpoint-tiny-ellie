If you are unable to bind an account in Pathpoint, there are five common causes. Check each one before contacting your Account Manager.

## 1. Missing Agency Appointment
Your agency must complete the appointment process before binding.

**Fix:** Complete your appointment from the dashboard or in Step One of the subjectivities workflow. Go to **Settings → Start Appointment** (takes about two minutes).

## 2. No PathPay Account Set Up
Your agency must establish a billing method through PathPay before binding is enabled.

**Fix:** Go to **Settings → Billing & Commissions** and create your PathPay account. Alternatively, set it up during Step One of the subjectivities workflow.

## 3. Missing Agent NPN (National Producer Number)
The National Producer Number is required to bind policies in most states.

**Fix:** Contact your Account Manager to have your NPN added to your file.

## 4. Expired Quote
Most carriers allow quotes to remain valid for 30–45 days. After expiration, the quote is no longer bindable.

**Fix:** Refresh the submission to obtain a new, current quote.

## 5. Incomplete or Rejected Subjectivities
All required forms must be properly signed and dated by both the agent and the insured.

**Fix:** Review the subjectivities checklist. Common issues include:
- Missing agent or insured signature
- Forms not dated
- Missing state-required digital stamps
- Wrong document uploaded

If a subjectivity was rejected, upload the corrected form and click **Resubmit**.
